# THATS OC CLT — Design System ("Queen City Editorial")

Codified so AI defaults can't creep back in. If it's not here, don't invent it.

## Principle
70% quiet-luxury editorial (Aimé Leon Dore / Kith lane) · 30% Charlotte street grit.
Photography + type + whitespace carry it. The graffiti logo is the ONE loud element.
Anti-AI rules: no all-caps headlines, no ✓/★ decoration spam, no hype copy, ≤3 colors
(neutrals + one accent), serif headlines, real voice.

## Color (neutrals + ONE accent; navy = dark-neutral; oxblood = rare alert)
- --paper  #F3EFE6  warm cream (page)
- --surface #FBF8F1  lifted cream (cards)
- --sink   #E6DFD0  image bg
- --ink    #1A1714  warm near-black (text)
- --muted  #6F685C  warm grey
- --faint  #A89F8E
- --teal   #0E8C82  the ONE accent (logo + Charlotte/Hornets) — used sparingly
- --navy   #1C2B3A  dark sections + footer (dark neutral)
- --blood  #7A2230  ONLY drop-live / sold states (replaces orange) — almost never
- NO ORANGE. NO purple. NO gradients-as-accent.

## Type (3 registers)
- Display/headline: **Fraunces** (serif), mixed case, weight 500–700, accent word = italic teal.
- Body/UI: **Hanken Grotesk**, sentence case.
- Technical only: **Space Mono** — SKU, COA #, size code, eyebrows, ticker. Small, wide-tracked.
- Headlines are NEVER all-caps. Eyebrows (tiny mono) may be uppercase.

## Voice
Understated, specific, confident. No "global heat lands here." Examples:
"The Queen City's source for verified pairs." / "Sourced and authenticated in Charlotte."
Sentence case. Let the product talk.

## Motif
Queen City crown (subtle), Plaza Midwood, "QC". Concrete grain for grit.
